How the Game-a-thon Works

The Kheyl is powering a 48-hour gaming marathon where participants can compete in a variety of popular games. Teams or individuals can register to participate, and the event will feature a mix of competitive tournaments and casual gaming sessions.


Scan the QR or click on it to join in!

  • Event Format

    The Kheyl will feature a mix of competitive tournaments and casual gaming sessions, allowing participants to showcase their skills and have fun.

  • Schedule

    The event will run for 36 hours, starting at 11 AM on Saturday and ending at 11 PM on Sunday.

  • Participant Requirements

    Participants must be at least 18 years old and have a valid email address to register for the event.
